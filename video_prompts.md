### Assembly & Export Notes

* **Total Duration:** Edit to roughly 36 seconds total (9 clips @ 4 seconds each).
* **Pacing:** Cuts every 4 seconds.
* **Color progression:** Color temperature literally warming shot-to-shot from cool blue to gold to cool night blue as the structural throughline.
* **Transition:** Cross-dissolve only at the loop point.
* **Formats & Export:**
  - **Desktop:** Web-optimized, target under 8 MB, 1920×1080 resolution.
  - **Mobile:** 720p variant.
  - **Poster Image:** Export the first frame as a poster image (`poster.jpg` / `poster.png`).
