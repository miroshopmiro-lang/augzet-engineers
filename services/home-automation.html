03 / Automation

Homes that think in kilowatts

IoT-based automation for home electrical equipment — lighting, climate and loads that respond intelligently, engineered for energy efficiency in green building projects.

Efficiency you don't have to think about

Automation is not a gadget layer — it is an energy strategy. Augzet designs IoT-based control for your home's electrical equipment so lights, fans, pumps and appliances draw power only when they should.

What the package includes

  • IoT-based control of lighting, climate and appliance circuits
  • Energy monitoring with usage visibility
  • Integration with solar generation for smart consumption
  • Green building project support and documentation
  • Client training — you understand and control the system you own

Built into the wiring, not taped onto it

Because we are electrical engineers first, automation is designed into the electrical system itself — documented, serviceable and ready for the loads you will add later.
