Flagship Delivery

From survey to subsidy

01 — Survey & Simulation

Measured twice. Modelled once.

Every project starts on the roof, not in a brochure. We survey the structure, shading and load profile, then build a software simulation with 3D drawings — so the forecast you sign is the one your meter delivers.

02 — Engineering & Installation

Certified products. Documented work.

Certified panels, engineered mounting, documented wiring. Our clients tell the same story in their reviews: no hidden charges, well planned, executed inside the promised time frame.

03 — Handover & Subsidy

Grid-synced. Subsidy secured.

We hand over a working plant with its full drawing set, register your MNRE subsidy — secured in as little as 40 days — and put the system on an annual maintenance plan. One point of contact, for the life of the plant.
